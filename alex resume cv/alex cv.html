About Me

Dedicated education professional with over 29 years of progressive experience in teaching, teacher development, and educational leadership. Proven expertise in curriculum design, teacher training, professional licensing, and quality assurance within the Somali Regional State Education Bureau.

Resume

Alemayehu Kebede Abebe has 29 years of experience in education as teacher, trainer, and leader. He worked as an elementary and secondary teacher across multiple districts before serving for decades in the Somali Regional State Education Bureau.

He led programs in teacher development, licensing & re-licensing, curriculum planning, and quality assurance. He has collaborated with the Ministry of Education, USAID, and UNICEF in capacity building and educational reforms.
